Find a Property.

You should be able to the county assessor's office online; go to tax information and put in the address for the property. The owner will be listed on the information that comes up. There is usually no charge for this information and it is very easy to find.

Once you know the area you want to live, you should start researching the properties in that area thoroughly so you really get to know the local market well.

Investing.
